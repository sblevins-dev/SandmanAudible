Sandman Act III

 James McAvoy reprises his role as Morpheus, the Lord of Dreams, in The Sandman, directing perhaps the most impressive audio cast ever assembled: Act Three Dirk Maggs, an award-winning audio pioneer, turns Neil Gaiman's groundbreaking DC graphic novel series into yet another fully immersive listening experience, this time in breathtaking 3D audio.

 We follow Morpheus on a grand journey to manage the family business in Act III. He pays a visit to Orpheus, his son, which has significant repercussions. He locates their estranged brother, Destruction, the only Endless member to ever leave their position. A storm that threatens to destroy reality itself brings a group of travelers together at an inn at the crossroads of all worlds, with serious consequences for Morpheus and the Endless.

It's time to listen with your eyes closed. Once more.
